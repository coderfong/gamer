How it works

Live in three steps. No code, no agency.

You bring the brand and the prizes. We handle the game, the capture, and the redemption loop.

1

Skin it to your brand

Drop in your logo, pick two colors, choose a vertical. Every game instantly matches your look — no designer needed.

2

Share a link or QR

Print a QR for the counter, drop the link in an email, or embed the game on your site. Players are one tap away.

3

Capture & reward

Collect the lead, hand out the prize, and track every redemption. Vouchers live on the player's phone until used.
